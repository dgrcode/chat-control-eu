## Excluded from overall-position classification

The March and July amendment bundles are documented in the official RCV annexes and HowTheyVote amendment pages. They are not converted into one `in favor`/`against` label without a ballot-specific analysis of exact text and operative effect. This prevents a vote for encryption exclusion or other privacy safeguard from being misrepresented as a general vote for the underlying law.

## Permanent CSA Regulation

No material European Parliament plenary roll-call vote was found for the permanent CSA Regulation procedure [2022/0155(COD)](https://oeil.europarl.europa.eu/oeil/en/procedure-file?reference=2022/0155(COD)) in either the 2019–2024 or 2024–2029 terms. The LIBE committee adopted A9-0364/2023 in November 2023, but its Rule 71 route produced no plenary roll-call vote; committee votes are excluded from this Parliament-wide MEP record.
