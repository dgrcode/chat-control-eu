# Attendance classification — 9 July 2026

**Official source:** [European Parliament attendance register for the 9 July 2026 Strasbourg sitting](https://www.europarl.europa.eu/doceo/document/PV-10-2026-07-09-ATT_EN.html).

The sitting contained two tracked ballots with different sets of non-voters. Their complete matching reports are kept separately:

- [Initial motion to reject the Council position](./attendance-2026-07-09-council-rejection.md)
- [Final motion to reject Parliament’s amended position](./attendance-2026-07-09-amended-rejection.md)

The historical table below is retained for the initial motion. The attendance register is sitting-level, not a timestamped record of chamber presence at the exact ballot.

- `no vote — present`: recorded present for the sitting, but no named vote on the ballot.
- `no vote — not recorded present`: no named vote and not recorded in the official sitting attendance register. This does not establish the reason, including whether an MEP was travelling, excused, or on holiday.

**Counts:** 32 present / 80 not recorded present, out of 112 no recorded votes.
