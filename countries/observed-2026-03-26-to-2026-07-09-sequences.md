# Observed vote sequences by country

This report identifies MEPs recorded `against` the Commission proposal on 26 March 2026 and then, on the 9 July initial motion to reject the Council position, recorded as `in favor`, `abstained`, `no vote — present`, or `no vote — not recorded present`. The July ballot was procedurally inverted: `in favor` means the MEP voted against rejecting the Council position.

July attendance classification comes from the [official attendance register](../sources/attendance-2026-07-09.md). `not recorded present` does not claim why an MEP did not vote.

**Count:** 153 MEPs across 26 countries.
